Foodinality Test

Rediscover Life Through Food

What if your food habits could reveal your life habits?

At the core of our project, we believe that how we approach food reflects how we navigate stress, emotion, and meaning.

Through the three cycles of eating—before, during, and after—and three driving forces—desire, routine, ethos—we uncover nine food personalities.

Each one holds clues to your emotional resilience, ecological sensitivity, cognitive control, and self-awareness.

In a world where urban living strips food down to function, re-encountering the full cycle of food—from selection to savoring, from waste to soil—is an act of rewilding our human experience.

We don’t just reveal where your pressure points are.
We offer you a way back:
starting from a worm bin, a seed, a bite—
to feel again the slow, powerful, living cycle you are part of.
